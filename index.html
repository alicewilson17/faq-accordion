FAQs

What is Flourish and Thrive?

Flourish and Thrive is a podcast that explores topics around mental health, well-being, personal development, and finding balance in life. The podcast is hosted by Alice and Emily, who discuss their personal experiences, offer practical advice, and share insights on how to thrive in various aspects of life, including career, relationships, and self-care.

The Flourish and Thrive podcast is currently available on Spotify and Apple Podcasts.
